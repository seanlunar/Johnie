day 2

The day starts early at 05:30hrs at the restaurant. Start the day with a cup of coffee/tea and some cereals. Go for another game drive at 06:00hrs

day 3

on the road trip back to Lilongwe It includes 3 game drives, two days park fees, meals, accommodation and transport to and fro Lilongwe.

CENTRAL AND NORTHERN EXPERIENCE

Malawi

  • Duration 10days and 9 nights
  • Airport Kamuzu International

10 DAYS CENTRAL NORTHEN EXPERIENCE

Day 1

Arrive Kamuzu International Airport and meet the ever smiling guide from Misuku Expeditions

day 2

Depart Lilongwe for Salima in the morning

day 3

boat trip around Nankhoma Island.Feed the fish eagles on our way back to the Hotel. leave for Kut wildlife Reserve

day 4

After a good breakfast depart Sengabay for Mua Museum in Dedza

day 5

After breakfast a visit to the Rock Paintings one of the heritage sites in Malawi

day 6

Depart Lilongwe for Mzuzu, the northern part of Malawi

day 7

Have breakfast, depart for Nyika National Park

day 8

Depart Nyika National Park for Nkhatabay.

day 9

Have breakfast; go for shopping around the beach where different amazing carvings are made.

day 10

Depart Kande Beach for Lilongwe
