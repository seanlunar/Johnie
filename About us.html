Misuku Expeditions

Misuku Expeditions a Malawian owned tour and safari company founded in 2013. Over the years the company has experienced some tremendous growth in terms of the customer base. Started as a one man show but now has a team of four full time employees and intends to increase the number in the few years. The company is based in Lilongwe, the capital city of Malawi, “The warm heart of Africa.” Most of the tours and safaris will start and end from because of Kamuzu International Airport the main airport for Malawi. Among the services offered by the company stands out the coordination of itineraries for individuals or groups, including hotel reservations, transport, tours, safaris, photographic tours or safaris, car rentals, guiding, logistical support activities and any other special requirements necessary. We always work thinking of you, of your needs, but above all, of your expectations. We know that every detail is important, and for this reason make our best effort to care for all those "little things" which will always make a big difference so that your trip will be exceptional. Our service style is characterized by being flexible and creative in the design of itineraries; for this we are diligent in the selection of our transport personnel, guides and all those persons involved in the operation. Our clients come from the United Kingdom, United States, Germany, Holland, Belgium, Italy, France, Spain, Cihina, South Korea, South Africa, Zambia among other countries and every day we expand our horizons to work with new markets from all over the world.
